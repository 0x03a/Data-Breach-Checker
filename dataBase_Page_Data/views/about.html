What is Secure Net?

Secure Net is a modern web application designed to help you protect your digital identity by checking if your passwords have been compromised in known data breaches. Our platform empowers users to:

  • Sign up and securely log in to your account
  • Check if your password has been breached
  • Update your profile and credentials
  • Benefit from strong password validation and email verification
  • Admins can manage users and monitor breach databases

Why use Secure Net?

With the increasing number of data breaches worldwide, it is crucial to ensure your passwords are not part of any known leaks. Secure Net provides a simple, user-friendly interface to check your credentials and stay safe online.

Features

  • User Authentication: Secure login and signup with robust validation
  • Password Breach Checking: Instantly check if your password is safe
  • Admin Dashboard: Manage users and breach data
  • Responsive Design: Works seamlessly on desktop and mobile

Our Mission

We aim to make the internet a safer place by empowering users to take control of their digital security. Your privacy and safety are our top priorities.
